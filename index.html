Frequently Asked Questions

What people want to know

What services do you offer as a web developer?

I offer a wide range of web development services including custom website design and development, responsive front-end development, back-end programming, e-commerce integration, website maintenance, and performance optimization. Whether you need a portfolio site, business landing page, or a full-scale web application, I can build tailored solutions that meet your goals and technical requirements.

Do you build mobile-friendly and responsive websites?

Yes, absolutely. Every website I create is fully responsive and mobile-friendly, meaning it will adapt seamlessly across all screen sizes—from smartphones and tablets to desktops. I use a mobile-first design approach and test across multiple devices to ensure optimal performance and user experience on every platform.

Which technologies do you use when developing websites?

I work with a variety of modern web technologies. For front-end development, I use HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and frameworks like Bootstrap and React. On the back-end, I commonly use PHP (with Laravel), Node.js, and MySQL for database management. I also integrate third-party APIs, CMS platforms like WordPress, and tools like Git for version control.

How long does it take to complete a website project?

The time frame depends on the scope and complexity of the project. A simple one-page website may take 3-5 days, while a full-featured e-commerce or business site may take 2–4 weeks. I always discuss deadlines in advance and provide a project timeline, ensuring timely delivery without compromising quality.

Do you offer ongoing website maintenance and support?

Yes, I provide ongoing support and maintenance packages to ensure your site remains updated, secure, and running smoothly. This includes regular backups, plugin updates, bug fixes, content updates, and performance monitoring. You’ll have peace of mind knowing your site is in good hands even after launch.

Can you help with SEO and website performance?

Definitely. I implement SEO best practices during development such as clean URL structures, fast loading times, mobile optimization, and semantic HTML. I also offer performance audits, speed enhancements, and integration with tools like Google Analytics and Search Console to help your website rank better and perform optimally.
